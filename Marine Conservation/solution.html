How to Prevent Ocean?

Reduce Your Use of Single-Use Plastics

We can opt for reusable alternatives. Reusable products are critical for environmental health and it can also be cost-effective. For example we can bring your own bag.




Restricted fishing

The government has promulgated and implemented a series of laws and policies to limit excessive marine capture.




Reduce your carbon emissions

We can drive less to reduce the carbon emissions. For example, walk, take public transportation, carpool or bike to your destination when possible.
